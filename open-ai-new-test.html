Togo FAQs

How long does expansion take?

The Togo sofa expands to its full size within 2-3 minutes after removing it from the box. No tools or assembly required.

Can I sit on it immediately after expansion?

Yes, the Togo is ready to use immediately after expansion. The foam regains its shape completely within minutes.

Is there any smell initially?

There might be a slight new product smell that dissipates within 24-48 hours when placed in a well-ventilated area.

Sofa Cum Bed FAQs

Is it suitable for daily sleeping?

Yes, our sofa cum bed is designed with a supportive mattress layer that makes it comfortable for daily sleeping.

How easy is it to convert from sofa to bed?

The conversion takes less than 30 seconds. Simply remove the back cushions and pull out the bed frame.

Does it need special bedsheets?

No, it uses standard bedsheet sizes (typically single or double bed size depending on the model).
